üéÆ General Questions

What is Space Shooter? +
Space Shooter is a fast-paced arcade-style space combat game where you battle through waves of alien enemies, collect power-ups, and fight epic bosses. With 20 unlockable maps, multiplayer support for up to 32 players, achievements, and daily challenges, it offers endless hours of entertainment!
Is the game free? +
Yes! Space Shooter is completely free to play. You can play in your browser or download the Windows/Linux versions at no cost. There are no hidden fees or paywalls.
Is the game safe to download? +
Yes, absolutely! The game is completely safe. Some antivirus software may show false positives because the game is not code-signed (which costs money). You can safely add it to your antivirus exceptions. We do not collect any personal data without your consent, and the game contains no malware or spyware.

üë§ Accounts & Saves

Do I need an account to play? +
No! You can play without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to:
  • Save your progress across devices
  • Compete on the global leaderboard
  • Unlock and track achievements
  • Access multiplayer features
  • Earn daily rewards and challenges
How do cloud saves work? +
When you create an account, your progress automatically syncs to the cloud. This means you can play on your desktop, switch to your laptop or phone, and continue exactly where you left off. Your high scores, achievements, and unlocked ships are all saved!
What if I lose my password? +
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

üöÄ Gameplay

How do I play the game? +
Controls:
  • Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Spacebar or Left Mouse Button
  • Pause: ESC or P
  • Special Ability: Shift
Check out our full Controls Guide for gamepad and mobile controls!
What are power-ups and how do they work? +
Power-ups are special items dropped by enemies that give you temporary abilities:
  • Shield: Protects you from damage
  • Rapid Fire: Increases your fire rate
  • Triple Shot: Shoot 3 bullets at once
  • Speed Boost: Move faster
  • Health: Restores your health
How many maps are there? +
Space Shooter features 20 unique maps including Asteroid Fields, Nebula Clouds, Ice Fields, Debris Zones, Deep Space, and more! Each map has unique visuals, enemy patterns, and challenges. Maps are unlocked as you progress through the game.
Can I play with friends? +
Yes! Space Shooter supports multiplayer with up to 32 players. You can play cooperatively with friends or compete for the highest score. Join or create a game lobby, invite your friends, and battle aliens together!

üîß Technical Issues

The game is laggy or running slowly. What can I do? +
Try these solutions:
  • Close other programs and browser tabs
  • Update your graphics drivers
  • Lower graphics quality in game settings
  • Play in fullscreen mode
  • Try the downloaded version instead of browser
  • Restart your device
The game won't load in my browser +
Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari). Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. If it still doesn't work, try the downloadable version for Windows or Linux.
My antivirus is blocking the download +
This is a false positive! The game is completely safe. Antivirus software sometimes flags unsigned executables. You can safely whitelist the game or download it from our trusted sources:
  • GitHub Releases
  • Itch.io Page
No sound or audio issues +
  • Check that your system volume is not muted
  • Check in-game audio settings
  • Make sure your browser allows audio
  • Try refreshing the page or restarting the game
  • Update your audio drivers

What are achievements? +
Achievements are special challenges and milestones you can complete to earn rewards and showcase your skills. Examples include reaching Wave 50, defeating 1000 enemies, unlocking all ships, and more. Check your profile to see your achievement progress!

Yes! Unlock different ships as you progress, each with unique stats and abilities. You can also unlock ship skins and customization options by completing achievements and challenges.
