About the Game

Space Shooter is an intense action-packed arcade game where you battle through endless waves of alien invaders across 20 unique maps. Collect power-ups, unlock ships, customize your experience, and compete with friends in multiplayer battles.

Developed by NEXO GAMES (King_davezz), this game combines classic arcade shooter mechanics with modern progression systems, including achievements, daily rewards, battle pass, and social features.
