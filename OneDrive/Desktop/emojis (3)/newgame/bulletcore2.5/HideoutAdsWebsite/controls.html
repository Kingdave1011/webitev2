üí° Pro Tips

  • Practice Makes Perfect: Spend time mastering basic movement and aiming before attempting harder waves
  • Use Both Hands: One hand for movement, one for firing gives you maximum control
  • Watch Enemy Patterns: Learn enemy movement and bullet patterns to predict attacks
  • Stay Mobile: Constantly moving makes you harder to hit
  • Power-up Priority: Collect Shield and Health power-ups first when in danger
  • Controller Comfort: Many players prefer controllers for smoother analog movement
  • Custom Bindings: Experiment with different key mappings to find what feels best for you
